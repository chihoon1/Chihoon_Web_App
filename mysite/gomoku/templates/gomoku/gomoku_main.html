About Gomoku

Gomoku is traditionally played with Go pieces (black and white stones) on a Go board.
Players alternate turns placing a stone of their color on an empty intersection. Black stone starts first.
The winner is the first player to form an unbroken chain of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
However, Placing so that a line of more than five stones of the same color is created does not result in a win.
There are various optional rules. Here, only the rule of three and three applies
The rule of three and three bans a move that simultaneously forms two open rows of three stones (rows not blocked by an opponent's stone at either end).
For more about Gomoku, Go Wikipedia
